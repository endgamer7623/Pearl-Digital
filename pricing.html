FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Still have questions? Our team responds within 24 hours.

Talk to Us →
Are you a real agency or just freelancers? +
Pearl Digital is a structured studio — not a freelancer marketplace. Every account is assigned a dedicated team that reports to a team lead. You have one point of contact, consistent delivery, and proper account management.
How do I know the quality will be good? +
Every specialist on our team completes a structured training programme and earns certification before working on any live account. We only assign certified marketers to client campaigns, and every deliverable goes through a quality review before it reaches you.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — monthly plans can be cancelled at the end of any billing period with no penalty. Annual plans are non-refundable but can be paused in exceptional circumstances. We'd rather earn your loyalty than lock you in.
How do you take payment? +
We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and international bank transfer via Stripe. Invoices are issued at the start of each billing cycle. We can also accommodate purchase orders for enterprise clients.
What's the onboarding process? +
After subscribing, you'll complete a 20-minute onboarding form covering your business, goals, audience and brand. Within 48 hours, your dedicated team reviews everything and books a kickoff call. Your first deliverables are live within 5–7 business days.
